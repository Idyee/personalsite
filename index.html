Short Bio

I studied BS in Physics at the University of Pretoria in South Africa. I am a self-taught software developer. I Founded a software and hardware company called Idyee that specialises in educational software. Whithin the company we establised a project called Reacoda that aims to introduce computer programming to 1 million people.

I am very passionate about education, technology, farming, Machine Learning (Artificial Intelligence) and Space exploration. I am particularly interested in introducing Computer programming to the masses. The same way we've been to read an write text is the same reason I think we should learn to read and write code.
